Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

Orders

How do I check my order status?

Check your order status online by entering the order number or logging in to your Nuts and bolts account. You’ll receive email updates if your order status changes.

How do I know my order is ready for pick up?

You will receive an email with pickup instructions and the location of the store you selected when your order is ready. You can pick up your order during store operating hours at the designated pickup area. Just provide the order confirmation email or order number with your photo ID (state driver's license, military ID or state ID). For your protection, only the cardholder or an authorized user of the credit card may pick up the order.

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel items from orders that haven’t yet shipped or haven't been picked up in store by signing into your Nuts and bolts’s account and selecting Orders & Purchases.

Do you have curbside pick up?

Yes, wwe have curbside pick up. All you have to do is order and chose curb side pick up when selecting the shipping method.

Products

How do I find products online?

You can find products by searching using a keyword, Nuts an bolts' SKU number, brand or manufacturer's model number. You can also browse by department to shop Nuts and bolts’s large selection of items.

How do I register for military discount?

Sign up for a Nuts and bolts' account to begin registration. Once you have a Nuts and bolts' account, sign in and click Get Started on the Military Discount banner.

How do I submit a rebate?

Visit the Rebate Center to submit and check the status of a rebate or to see current rebate offers for thousands of Nuts and bolts' products.

Do you offer rental services?

Yes. We provide many rental options. Please visit your nearest Nuts and Bolts store to find your perfect tools.

Policy Statement

What is the Nuts and Bolts' return policy?

Merchandise may be returned within 90 days for a full refund or replacement with receipt.

When returning an item to a store, the store manager has the right to refuse returns of any item, at any time, for any reason.

What is Nuts and Bolts warranty policy?

Nuts and bolts makes every effort to meet high quality and durability.

Nuts and bolts offers a 90-day warranty on defects in materials and workmanship beginning on the date of purchase.

This warranty does not apply to damage due directly or indirectly, to misuse, abuse, negligence or accidents, repairs or alterations outside our facilities, criminal activity, improper installation, normal wear, and tear, or to lack of maintenance.

What is the private policy?

Nuts and bolts will not share, sell, or distributes your personal information.

What is the store's covid 19 policy?
